Wine Cocktails & Brunch Bevs...

...AKA, drinks you can enjoy while the sun is still high in the sky! For these, you'll obviously need to make sure you have wine. I recommend LaMarca Prosecco and Simonet champagne wherever bubbles are required.

Classic Mimosa

Ingredients

  • Champagne
  • Orange juice

How to make it:

Some people like a mimosa in a pint glass over ice, some people like it sans ice in a champagne flute. Either way, you're going to fill the glass most of the way with the sparkling wine, then top it off with a splash of orange juice.

Enjoy!

Mimosas are often enjoyed by the pitcher. To make a pitcher of mimosas, simply pour the whole bottle of bubbly into a pitcher, add a splash of OJ, and you're good to go. Share with whoever you're quarantined with... or don't.

Image at right courtesy of Joe Shlabotnik on Flickr, CC BY-SA 2.0.

Variations
  • Add 1 to 2 ounces of pomegranate liqueur to a pitcher, or just a dash to a glass. This is my favorite way to drink a mimosa- the tartness of the pomegranate cuts the sweetness of the orange juice in such a perfect way.
  • Use cranberry juice instead of OJ- this variation is called a Pointsettia!
  • Use grapefruit juice instead of OJ- then add a shot of elderflower liqueur!

The French 75

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of gin
  • half-ounce of lemon juice (about half a lemon, squeezed)
  • half-ounce of simple syrup
  • Prosecco or champagne (I prefer prosecco)

How to make it:

  1. Mix the lemon juice and simple syrup in a vessel and adjust to your taste- add more simple syrup if you need to.
  2. Add the gin and lemon-syrup mixture to a champagne flute. Top with prosecco or champagne.
  3. Enjoy!
Variation
  • Substitute elderflower liqueur for the gin if you want less alcohol and more of a floral flavor!

Aperol Spritz

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces of Aperol
  • 3 ounces of Prosecco
  • Club soda

How to make it:

  1. Add ice to a wine glass. Add the Aperol and Prosecco, then top off the glass with club soda.
  2. Enjoy!
Variations
  • Elderflower spritz: Use an elderflower liqueur instead of Aperol.
  • Pomegranate spritz: Use a pomegranate liqueur instead of Aperol.

Bloody Mary

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of vodka
  • 1 wedge of lemon
  • 1 wedge of lime
  • For the mix: 4 ounces of tomato juice, hot sauce (to your preference), Worcestershire sauce (to your preference), black pepper, and smoked paprika.

How to make it:

  1. Squeeze the lemon and lime wedges into a shaker, then fill the shaker with ice.
  2. Add the ingredients to the shaker, shake gently (do not overmix), and strain it into a glass filled with fresh ice.
  3. Enjoy!
Variation
  • Bloody Maria: Substitute tequila for vodka.

Irish Coffee

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of Irish whiskey
  • Fresh coffee
  • Whipped cream (optional)

How to make it:

  1. Pour the whiskey into a mug. Top with hot coffee.
  2. Enjoy!
Variations
  • Use a cream liqueur instead of whiskey- I'm especially fond of Bailey's and coffee.
  • The Cold Irish- iced coffee with a shot of Irish cream. Perfect for a summer brunch!
