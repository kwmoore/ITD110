Got the quarantine blues?

Do you miss going out for a carefree drink with friends without having to worry about getting sick? Same here.

The food and beverage industry has been hit hard by COVID-19. Although bars and restaurants are beginning to re-open after being shuttered since March, there remains uncertainty about how safe it really is to go out right now. Not to mention many of us have suffered a hit to our income during the pandemic and going out in Northern Virginia has always been expensive.

Here at Lockdown Cocktails, it is our mission to provide delicious drink recipes that anyone can make at home, no matter your level of expertise. Whether you already have a well-stocked home bar or you're looking to get one set up, we got you covered.

Remember: Alcohol is a solution!
